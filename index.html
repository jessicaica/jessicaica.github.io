My Portfolio

Hi! My name is Jessica Sy, and this website is my own website to showcase my current art portfolio! My passion for art started when I was a child, and has followed me throughout my life. I want to use this website to show my growth through my art within the recent years, and as well be a space for me to share my art. Art is a big passion of mine, whether its watercolor, or photography, or even creating sculptures! I've always enjoyed making art and sharing with other people.
